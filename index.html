Inside My Internship Experience

DepEd Sorsogon City Division


The Department of Education (DepEd) Sorsogon City Division is committed to delivering quality, equitable, culture-based, and inclusive basic education for every learner.

As an intern, I’m thankful for the chance to be part of this mission by helping with different IT-related tasks and projects that make the office’s work more efficient.

Through this internship journal, I document my experiences, learnings, and contributions as I work with the DepEd Sorsogon City Division. This serves as a reflection of my growth and a record of how I apply my skills to real-world projects and challenges.

About Me

RODRIGO S. BELESARIO III

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology 4

Hi! I’m a fourth-year BS Information Technology student at The Lewis College, based in Sirangan, Sorsogon City. I’m passionate about technology and enjoy creating efficient, user-friendly websites.

I have experience in both front-end and back-end development, managing databases, and designing intuitive user interfaces. I find it very rewarding to collaborate with teams to solve real-world problems through technology.

During my internship, I aim to apply what I’ve learned in school, contribute meaningfully to team projects, and gain hands-on experience. This journal documents my journey, experiences, and the projects I work on throughout this period.

Internship Journal

WEEK 1

Getting Started

September 8 - September 12

My first week as an intern at the DepEd Sorsogon City Division was both exciting and a little nerve-wracking. Initially, I felt awkward moving around since I didn’t know the staff yet, but they were very kind and welcoming. I had the opportunity to meet Sir Francis, the ICT head, and I immediately learned a lot from him.

WEEK 2

Getting Into the Flow

September 15 - September 19

This is my second week as an intern at DepEd Sorsogon City Division. This week, we photocopied almost 864 pages of lists to be given to the teachers, and we’re still receiving memos and uploading them to WordPress.
